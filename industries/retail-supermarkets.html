Common Challenges

Challenges we understand

Our Approach

How Daira helps

Daira sets up a scalable close process for retail: POS-to-bank reconciliation, clean sales mapping, and reliable monthly reporting. We structure inventory and shrinkage reporting to protect margins and improve accuracy of COGS. We support VAT compliance with proper tax mapping and schedules aligned to POS categories and purchase documentation. We identify spend and margin leakage and propose a prioritized optimization roadmap (supplier terms, rebates tracking, overhead control).

Deliverables

What we deliver

POS reconciliation workflow (sales, discounts, returns, tender types, settlements)
Inventory/COGS reporting structure with shrinkage and write-off tracking
Gross margin reporting by category and store (where available)
Supplier rebate/incentive tracking schedule (if applicable)
VAT schedules aligned to POS item categories and purchase invoices
Spend analysis (top categories/vendors) with savings opportunities
Cash flow view focused on inventory purchases, supplier terms, and working capital
