The Finance Gap Holding SMEs Back

If you run an SME in the UAE or Bahrain, you have probably experienced at least one of these: books that are always a month behind, a VAT deadline that creeps up before the numbers are ready, or a growth decision you had to make on gut feeling because there was no reliable financial picture to work from.

The usual options do not fix this. A full-time CFO is too expensive. Freelance bookkeepers lack consistency. Generic software gives you tools but no guidance. And the big firms are not built for a business doing AED 100,000 a month.

That is the gap Daira was built to close.

So, What Is Daira?

Daira is an AI-powered financial services provider based in Dubai, with operations in UAE and Bahrain. We combine experienced finance professionals with smart technology to deliver accounting, tax compliance, financial dashboards, planning, and cost optimisation — all under one roof.

Think of it as a complete finance function for your business, without the enterprise price tag. You get a dedicated team, modern tools, and real-time visibility into your numbers. Plans start at AED 599 per month.

Who Is Daira For?

Daira serves SME founders, CEOs, and finance managers across the UAE and Bahrain. If your business has outgrown basic bookkeeping but is not ready for a full finance team, you are exactly who we built this for.

We have deep expertise in:

  • Construction — project costing, WIP tracking
  • Real estate — rent rolls, service charges
  • Restaurants — POS reconciliation, food cost analytics
  • Auto parts & trading — inventory, COGS, margins
  • Retail — high-volume reconciliation, margin visibility
  • Other sectors — financial visibility

But our services work for any SME that needs professional financial management.
