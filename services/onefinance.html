Our Solution

How Daira helps

OneFinance™ is your CFO command center — a financial analytics dashboard built for UAE SMEs. It combines five powerful modules: Cockpit (your at-a-glance financial overview), Health Score (a 0–100 rating with peer benchmarking), Financial Statements (structured views of your P&L, balance sheet, and more), Financial Reporting (automated KPI reports and analytics), and CashFlow Forecasting (forward-looking cash projections). Plus, the built-in Affordability Calculator lets you model what-if scenarios — new hires, office expansion, equipment purchases — so you can decide with confidence. All powered by the Excel data you already have.
